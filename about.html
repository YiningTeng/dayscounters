About CountToday

CountToday is a small informational website focused on residency-day calculations for people tracking time spent in the United States or Canada. Our goal is to make these calculations easier to understand with simple browser-based tools and plain-language reference content.

What We Publish

We publish calculator tools and supporting guides that help visitors estimate days for travel history review, residency planning, immigration preparation, and tax-related record keeping. Our site currently includes:

  • an I-94-based U.S. days counter,
  • a Canada days counter for self-entered dates,
  • a Canada days counter based on I-94 history, and
  • supporting reference guides about U.S. and Canadian residency concepts.

How Our Tools Work

CountToday tools are designed to run in your browser. The travel history or date ranges you paste into the calculators are processed locally on your device for the purpose of displaying results on the page.

Important: CountToday is for educational and planning purposes only. Our tools do not replace legal, tax, immigration, or professional advice, and visitors should always confirm important results with official sources.

Our Content Approach

We aim to provide practical, original publisher content alongside the calculators so visitors can understand what the calculations mean, where the underlying travel data comes from, and how to verify results with official government resources.
